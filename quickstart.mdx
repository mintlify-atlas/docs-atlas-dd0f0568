title: "Quickstart"

---

The PF2e system is available directly from Foundry's built-in system browser. This guide gets you from zero to your first session in about ten minutes.

<Note>
  The PF2e system requires **Foundry VTT v13** (minimum 13.348, verified on 13.351). Older Foundry versions are not supported by the current release.
</Note>

## Install the system

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your Foundry VTT setup screen">
    Navigate to your Foundry VTT installation and open the **Game Systems** tab in the Setup screen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install PF2e from the system browser">
    Click **Install System**, then search for **"Pathfinder Second Edition"** in the filter box. Click **Install** next to the official PF2e entry.

    Alternatively, paste the manifest URL directly into the **Manifest URL** field and click **Install**:

    ```
    https://raw.githubusercontent.com/foundryvtt/pf2e/v13-dev/system.pf2e.json
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new world">
    Go to the **Game Worlds** tab and click **Create World**. Set the **Game System** to **Pathfinder Second Edition**, give it a name, then click **Create World**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch the world and create a character">
    Click **Launch World** to enter it. Once inside:

    1. Open the **Actors** sidebar tab (the people icon).
    2. Click **Create Actor**, choose type **Character**, and give it a name.
    3. The character sheet opens — use the **ABC** tab to set Ancestry, Background, and Class by dragging entries from the Compendium.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse the Compendium for content">
    The **Compendium Browser** gives you fast, filtered access to all available content — spells, feats, bestiary creatures, equipment, and more.

    Open it from the **Compendium** sidebar tab, or by clicking the magnifying glass icon at the top of most selection dialogs on character sheets.

    Use the tabs across the top of the browser to filter by content type, then drag entries directly onto actor sheets or into the world.
  </Step>
</Steps>
