## What is PF2e for Foundry VTT?

The **PF2e system** is a fully-featured implementation of Pathfinder Second Edition rules for [Foundry Virtual Tabletop](https://foundryvtt.com). Built and maintained by a passionate volunteer community, it automates virtually every aspect of PF2e gameplay — from complex action economies and condition tracking to full spellcasting workflows and compendium-backed character building.

</Info>

## Key capabilities

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Actor Types" icon="users">
    Characters, NPCs, familiars, hazards, vehicles, parties, and armies — each with tailored data models and sheets.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Item Types" icon="swords">
    Spells, feats, ancestries, classes, equipment, conditions, effects, and 30+ more item categories.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rule Elements" icon="gears">
    A flexible rules engine that lets items modify actor statistics, grant abilities, and automate mechanical effects.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compendium Library" icon="books">
    Hundreds of packs covering bestiary monsters, spells, feats, equipment, adventures, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automated Rolls" icon="dice-d20">
    Full check and damage automation with degree-of-success calculation, modifiers, and roll notes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Homebrew Support" icon="flask">
    Extend the system with custom traits, damage types, languages, and other homebrew content.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
